Delicious Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Accara

    Deep-fried black-eyed pea fritters, seasoned with onions and spices, often served with a spicy dipping sauce.

  • Sambusa

    A popular West African snack, these are deep-fried pastries filled with spiced meat or vegetables.

Main Courses

  • Thieboou Dieune

    A traditional Senegalese dish made with fish, rice, and a variety of vegetables, all cooked in a flavorful tomato sauce.

  • Yassa Guinar

    A flavorful Senegalese dish made with marinated chicken, onions, and lemon, served with rice.

  • Vegetable Stir-Fry

    A medley of fresh vegetables stir-fried in a savory sauce, served with rice.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Thiakry

    A traditional Senegalese dessert made from millet couscous mixed with sweetened condensed milk, yogurt, and flavored with vanilla or cinnamon.

  • Beignets

    Deep-fried dough pastries, often dusted with powdered sugar, popular in Senegal and other parts of West Africa.
